<p><b>Stora Sundby Castle</b> (<a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <i lang="sv">Stora Sundby slott</i>) is a castle in <a href="/wiki/Eskilstuna_Municipality" title="Eskilstuna Municipality">Eskilstuna Municipality</a>, <a href="/wiki/S%C3%B6dermanland_County" title="Södermanland County">Södermanland County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a>.
The architectural features of the castle resembles those of a calendar, four large towers symbolizing the seasons, twelve small towers symbolizing the months of the year, 52 rooms symbolizing the weeks of the year, and a window for each day of the year (365). The architect was <a href="/wiki/Peter_Frederick_Robinson" title="Peter Frederick Robinson">Peter Frederick Robinson</a>.
</p>
