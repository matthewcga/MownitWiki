Laskowo [lasˈkɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rogoźno, within Oborniki County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Rogoźno, 21 km (13 mi) north-east of Oborniki, and 45 km (28 mi) north of the regional capital Poznań.
