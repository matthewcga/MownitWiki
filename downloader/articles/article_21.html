Myrtle Creek (Curry County, Oregon)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Myrtle Creek is a small stream, about 3 miles (5 km) long, in Curry County in the U.S. state of Oregon. It arises in the Siskiyou Mountains and flows generally southwest to its confluence with Mussel Creek in the Arizona Beach State Recreation Site. The two creeks flow under U.S. Highway 101 and through the park, where they merge and enter the Pacific Ocean about 10 miles (16 km) south of Port Orford.[1][2][3]
